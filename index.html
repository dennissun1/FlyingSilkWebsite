About Flying Silk

Flying Silk is a dance group at UNC Chapel Hill that specializes in Chinese ribbon and fan dance. Our goal is to spread Chinese dance and culture through our performances and build a community of students that are interested in Chinese ribbon and fan dance. We stress that NO previous dance experience is necessary, and encourage everyone to come to workshops and auditions if they are interested.
